Topic:
The Decline of Traditional Traffic Sources

Summary:
Rand discusses how platforms increasingly keep users within their ecosystems, reducing the amount of traffic sent to external websites. Marketers need to adapt by focusing on audience ownership, brand building, and multi-channel distribution strategies.

Key Insights:

Organic traffic is becoming harder to earn.
Platforms prioritize keeping users on-platform.
Audience ownership is increasingly important.
Strong brands are less dependent on algorithms.
