# Research Insights — B2B SaaS Marketing

## Key Patterns Found Across Experts

### 1. Clarity beats complexity
Most high-performing marketing strategies focus on clear, simple messaging rather than complex frameworks.

### 2. Consistency is more important than virality
Long-term posting consistency drives audience growth more than occasional viral content.

### 3. Strong positioning increases conversion
Brands that clearly define who they help and how they win outperform generic messaging.

### 4. Distribution is as important as content creation
Content without distribution strategy has limited impact.

### 5. Problem-focused messaging performs best
Content that directly addresses audience pain points consistently outperforms feature-based messaging.

---

## Cross-Expert Overlaps

- All experts emphasize audience understanding
- Most rely on simple frameworks over complex systems
- Personal branding is a major growth lever in B2B SaaS
- Repetition of message improves trust and recall

---

## Emerging Insight

B2B SaaS growth is less about creating more content and more about:
- positioning
- clarity
- repetition
- distribution
