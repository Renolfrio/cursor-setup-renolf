Post 1

Date: June 2026

Topic:
Create Once, Distribute Forever

Summary:
Ross argues that most marketers focus too much on creating new content and not enough on distributing existing content. He explains that valuable content often fails because it is published once and then forgotten. Instead, marketers should continuously repurpose and redistribute their best-performing assets.

Key Insights:

Distribution is as important as content creation.
Great content can generate value long after publication.
Repurposing increases content ROI.
Consistent promotion improves reach and results.

Post 3

Date: 2026

Topic:
Clarity Beats Efficiency in the AI Era

Summary:
Ross explains that AI has made content production easier than ever, reducing the competitive advantage of efficiency. In a world where everyone can generate content quickly, brands that stand out are those with clear positioning, strong messaging, and unique perspectives.

Key Insights:

AI makes content creation accessible to everyone.
Clear positioning creates differentiation.
Human judgment remains a competitive advantage.
Brand clarity builds trust and long-term growth.
